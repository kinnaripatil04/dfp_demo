About DFP


Long distance relationship with parents has its own challenges. Now that distance might be continents apart or cities apart or even buildings apart, you might be away from your parents due to job, relocated after marriage or be it any other reason, it’s challenging nonetheless. Your parents depend on you for a lot of things such as renewing a policy, paying the mobile bill, taking doctor’s appointments, getting monthly groceries, online shopping, booking cab etc. But considering our hectic work schedules it is difficult to be available for parents at all times. DFP knows this and want to make life easier for both parents and children. We aim to help caregiving children and their parents in the best possible way. We hope we are able to take some load off the children's shoulders and make their parents' life a bit more convenient.
